About us

Hero International Trading was founded in 2020.  The main business of the company is seafood trading. We have good business with many large-scale seafood suppliers locally and around the world, in order to acquire sustained and quality supply of seafood resources. We supply quality seafood to the segment of the industries ranges from wholesale distributors, restaurants, seafood eateries, caterers, factories, fast food chains, bulk purchase and local customers. We are in the frozen seafood industry that will provide you the superior quality seafood products. Maintaining the highest possible quality is an integral part of the Hero International Trading’s tradition.

We ensure that all products marketed by Hero International Trading are superior quality. With more than 10 years experience and expertise in the seafood industry, Hero International Trading has the facilities, distribution infrastructure and skill manpower to provide frozen seafood supply needs for all customer establishments. This enables better control of product quality, delivery logistic and cost efficiency. The challenges and complication on seafood are on the handling, storage and inconsistent supply. We able to provide solution to customers by repacking, processing and ease for storage in order to minimise and overcome the impact during the period of shortage. We valued our customers and suppliers business relationships which hold an inseparable bond to growth and pursue prosperity together.
